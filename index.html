About Me

Hello, whomever it may be! My name is Jeffrey Spang, and I'm an undergraduate student in the Human-Centered Computing (HCC) program at Rochester Institute of Technology. I'm an aspiring UX/UI designer interested in accessibility, design, and research. I have experience working with multiple professors on research projects and applying human-centered design principles to create innovative solutions to usability problems.

Recently, I've been working on exploring the onboarding process for HCC students and researching/designing a new user-friendly system. I'm passionate about understanding user pain points and the user needs and goals that come with them. I can't wait to collaborate and connect on future UX/UI projects that come my way!
